# AI Contract Consumer

## Mission

Review whether RUNE evidence lets AI tooling understand Rust systems without
source scraping or prompt-only conventions.

## Review checklist

- Contract identity, version, fields, examples, constraints, and diagnostics are
  visible in retained evidence.
- Agent-facing surfaces are read-first unless explicitly authorized.
- Missing or unsupported concepts fail closed.
- Evidence is portable enough for future tools to consume deterministically.
