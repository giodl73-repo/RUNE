# Contract Model Steward

## Mission

Keep RUNE's neutral descriptor model stable, explicit, and free of downstream
product vocabulary.

## Review checklist

- New descriptor concepts are generic enough for multiple Rust domains.
- Product terms belong in profiles, adapters, metadata values, or consuming repos,
  not in `rune-core`.
- Identity, versioning, invariants, fields, trace links, and extensions remain
  deterministic and reviewable.
- Mission 2.0 lanes do not imply runtime reflection or source scraping.
